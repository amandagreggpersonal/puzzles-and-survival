Reservoir War Tips

Only HQ 18 and higher can participate.

Make sure that you finish gathering before the event starts.
Your troops will end up at the state hospital if they were injured. They will be fully returned after the event. If you need to heal during the event, it only requires speed-ups. But if you are not attacking and are just filling buildings, it is better not to waste the speed-ups.
The purpose of this event is, to conquer/occupy as much buildings as possible. The buildings will open up in different stages. First the towers, second the hospitals and military buildings, third logistics building and last the Doomtower.
When you enter the event, your Headquarters will be ported to a safe zone. In this zone, you can’t be attacked, but you can send out marches to reinforce or attack buildings.
The biggest HQ’s should try to direct port and defend buildings and/or conquer buildings where the watertruck is going to pass and the the one with the biggest rally should occupy the doom first. The people occupying the doom with the best T’s + buildings where the watertruck passes, give the most points.
The smaller HQ’s should try to reinforce a building so that the buildings are occupied. The 3 towers closest to the safezone will be occupied first.
Keep some marches available to occupy the hospital and the military building for advantages during the game. After a few more minutes the doomtower will be unlocked. That tower should be reinforced as much as possible.
If you notice that your building is under attack, place the coordinates in the chat and call for reinforcements.
In reservoir war it is needed to win a few events otherwise, the logistics buildings will not unlock. Only if we climb up, and win a few events, the logistics towers will become available. Logistic buildings give first 5 direct ports and then every minute 1 port. But without the building, direct ports aren’t free. So be careful if you use them.
After the event, we’ll get some waterbottles. These waterbottles can be traded for Levina fragments. Levina is a very intersting Hero because her boosts are passive so try to get as many fragments as possible.
